3-D Printing in Manufacturing & Industry

Source 1

https://ultimaker.com/learn/benefits-of-3d-printing-in-manufacturing/ Ultimaker (industry resource) 3-D printing, also called additive manufacturing, is transforming how industries create and produce parts. Ultimaker explains that additive manufacturing improves efficiency by reducing lead times, lowering costs, and enabling rapid prototyping. Manufacturers can produce specialized components, jigs, and fixtures on demand, which minimizes downtime and streamlines assembly processes. 3-D printing also supports distributed production models, allowing companies to manufacture parts closer to the point of use. By reducing reliance on traditional tooling and inventory, businesses can respond faster to customer needs and supply chain disruptions. This technology is especially valuable for companies that need small batches or customized products without expensive equipment. Overall, 3-D printing provides measurable advantages in productivity, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness across industries.
